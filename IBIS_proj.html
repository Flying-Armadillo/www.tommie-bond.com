PROJECT NAME

I was tasked to educate people on the obscure history of the Australian white Ibis. A short educational immersive (360) video was chosen as the method of delivery as is a novel new medium that uniquely allows the video to convey greater sense of scale and audience interaction.
I reimbursed the beautiful models for their time and likeness, using the bread rolls.
The immersive video was filmed using the GoPro and the special audio was recorded with the H2n.
The script was researched, written and the recorded.
Premiere Pro was used to edit, combine, and compile the finish video which was then uploaded to YouTube.
